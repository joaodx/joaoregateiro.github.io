Biography

I am is a PhD Student in the Centre for Vision, Speech and Signal Processing at the University of Surrey. The title of my reseach is Video-based Character Animation in Immersive Environments, where I will address the problem of video realistic character animation and real-time interaction in immersive augmented and virtual enviroments.

My research interest is in 3D Computer Vision/Graphics, Multi-view Performance Capture, Human Computer Interection and Video-Based Character Animation to facilitate applications in film and gaming.

I have joined Vertual Ltd in 2014 to develop Virtual Reality simulation software in radiation therapy. Previously, I received my M.Sc. degree from the University of Hull (UK).
